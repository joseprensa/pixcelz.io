What doses pixcelz represent

Pixcelz represents the fusion of graphic art and NFT technology. Our collection of handmade NFTs embodies creativity, originality, and uniqueness in every pixel. Each piece of art is carefully designed and represents a piece of our passion for visual expression.
Pixcelz also symbolizes the connection between generations, since it is a project that I share with my daughter and that unites us in our passion for digital art. We want to create a community where both young and old can appreciate and collect our NFTs, providing a unique and exciting viewing experience.
Furthermore, Pixcelz stands for innovation and exploration of new forms of digital art through blockchain technology. By using the technology of NFTs, we give collectors the opportunity to own and display unique pieces in the virtual world.
